About Projects

My goal is to work towards a world where robots are able to help people achieve goals. By deploying functional robots, I believe we can greatly improve our society. I am focused on how we can use robotics to in green tech.


My areas of focus in robotics are planning and controls, reliable operation, and integrating hardware and software. I like to work on robots that change their environment. Understanding contact, the implications, and modelling of the changing environment are fascinating challengs.


I have implemented complex planning of heavy machinery through changing environments with noisy sensors. Deploying these algorithms and monitoring them gave me an appreciation for how good theoretical choices can crete a solid basis upon which we can build additional safeguards and short cuts to achieve high quality performance. For faster picking systems, I have developed pick plans that are time sensitive and achieve consistent performance even with noisy inputs from machine learning models and a control system that provides minimal feedback.


When I am not working on and dreaming about robots, I like to travel. I enjoy seeing people and places so different from me that it opens my mind up to the possibility of what could be.
