About Projects

I am interested in how robotics can improve our daily lives. I think we have a lot to gain from automating boring tasks and leaving ourselves to deal with the more interesting things. My primary interest in robotics is in control and planning for manipulation tasks. I find the challenge of dealing with a wide variety of objects and tasks complicated by noise from sensors and actuators an interesting challenge. In general, I just want to make robots do useful things.

I really enjoy making things. I commonly take things apart and unsuccessfully put them back together. I usually damage parts and components. I enjoy every second of it. And once in a while, I even make something cool.

I love how software and hardware can be combined to complete physical tasks. My fascination with robotics is based in achieving useful movements with machines.

When I am not working on and dreaming about robots, I like to travel. I enjoy seeing people and places so different from me that it opens my mind up to the possibility of what could be.

I want to work on projects that can change the world in real ways. I don't want to work on projects for the rich, but instead projects that can revolutionize an entire society.
