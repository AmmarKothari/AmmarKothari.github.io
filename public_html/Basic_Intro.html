Future Roboticist and PhD Candidate
Robotics Department
Oregon State University


I am interested in how robotics can improve our daily lives. I think we have a lot to gain from automating boring tasks and leaving ourselves to deal with the more interesting things, like exploring the galaxy, curing all cancers, and especially having more fun.

I also love to travel. I enjoy seeing people and places so different from me that it opens my mind up to the possibility of what could be.

I really enjoy making things. I commonly take things apart and unsuccessfully put them back together. I usually damage parts and components. I enjoy every second of it. And once in a while, I even make something cool.

I am from Northern California originally. The Bay Area is one of my favorite places on the planet. I love the Silicon Valley vibe. I am a passionate supporter of all things Cal, but am finding some room for the Beavers also!
